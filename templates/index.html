Good to know

What ends up on each card?

Product photo, title, SKU and a price badge — every card linking to your store. Discount versions show the old price crossed out.

What does Premium add?

Category divider pages built from your Shopify tags, compact 20 or 30-per-page layouts, crossed-out compare-at prices, and stock counts on every card.

Is my data stored?

Only long enough to build your PDF. Uploads and finished files are deleted automatically within two hours.
